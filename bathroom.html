BATHROOM DESIGN

Here’s introducing HomeLane’s personalized bathroom range, NERA. It is a custom curated and ergonomically designed range of bathrooms that suit your individual purposes, space requirements, and design preferences. Ergonomics is a science that deals with the study of the interaction of individuals with the surrounding environment. In the bathroom context, ergonomics refer to the particulars and specifications one needs to have in mind before formulating bathroom designs. Depending on the existing decor theme of your house, the NERA modules can be modified keeping in mind individual designs and requirements. Customers can choose from 100+ different material finishes and colours. The cabinets are constructed out of premium quality BWP ply, which is heat, water, and termites resistant, making them the perfect pick for every bathroom.
